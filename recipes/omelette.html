Omelette Recipe

The Omelette

Made from beaten eggs and filled with vegetables, meat, and/or cheese, this dish makes for an easy and quick meal preparation!! The omelette makes for a good breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese, any kind
  • 3 to 4 cherry tomatoes, cut in half and sprinkled lightly with salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil, parsley, or herb of your choice

Directions

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs using a fork.
  2. Melt the butter using a skillet over medium to low heat.

  3. Add the eggs to the skillet. Wait until the edges begin to set, then use a spatula to push the edges toward the center and tilt the pan so the uncooked eggs move to the edge.

    Repeat until eggs are somewhat set with a little softness in the center, about 6 min.

  4. Place cheese, tomatoes, herbs, and any filling of your choice down the center. Make sure not to overfill.
  5. Cook for about 1 minute longer, then use a spatula to slide under the eggs and to carefully fold the omelette.
  6. Use the spatula to place the omelette on a plate. There you go.

Microwaved Egg Sandwich Recipe

The Microwaved Egg Sandwich

If it isn't obvious yet, this recipe makes use of a microwave!!

Perfect for a breakfast meal, this egg sandwich can be made in minutes. If you are feeling somewhat lazy during the morning, this could be your go-to breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablspoon milk
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 slice American cheese

Directions

  1. Whisk the egg and milk in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook for 1-2 minutes in the microwave or until cooked through.
  3. While eggs are cooking, toast the bread.
  4. Transfer the cooked egg onto a slice of toasted bread.
  5. Add cheese and top it with the other slice of toasted bread.
  6. Cook in microwave until cheese is melted, about 15 seconds. There you go.
